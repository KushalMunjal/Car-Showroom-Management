Company History

  • 2019  -

  • AutoExpress Car Dealership was established in 2019 with a mission to provide high-quality vehicles and exceptional customer service to its clients. In its first year of operation, the company focused on building a solid foundation by establishing relationships with suppliers and manufacturers, hiring experienced staff, and investing in state-of-the-art technology and equipment to enhance the buying and service experience

  • 2020  -

  • In its second year, AutoExpress Car Dealership expanded its inventory to include a wider range of vehicles, from economical models to luxury brands, and began offering financing options to make it easier for customers to purchase the car of their dreams. The company also launched a website and social media presence to increase its online visibility and engage with customers.

  • 2021  -

  • By its third year, AutoExpress Car Dealership had established a reputation as a trusted source for quality vehicles and outstanding service. The company expanded its reach by opening a second location in a neighboring city, and introduced new services such as on-site maintenance and repairs, detailing, and car rentals

  • 2022  -

  • In its fourth year of operation, AutoExpress Car Dealership continued to innovate and adapt to meet the changing needs of its customers. The company launched a mobile app that allowed customers to browse inventory, schedule service appointments, and receive real-time updates on their vehicle status. The company also partnered with local charities and non-profits to give back to the community and build strong relationships with its neighbors.
